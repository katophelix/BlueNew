Meet the Fleet

Boomerang

Boomerang is a 32-foot Stuart Angler. The Boomerang is equipped with the latest electronics and safety equipment and is operated by David Spar, a U.S. Coast Guard licensed captain. There is a restroom on board, as well as shaded seating. Come and experience an exciting day of fishing aboard the Boomerang.

Miss Carolina

Capt. Rodney is a lowcountry native with over 20 years experience fishing and navigating the beautiful backwaters of our island. He can educate you on the history of the islands and various marine life you will encounter while you reel in the “catch of the day.” Dolphin sightings occur daily aboard the Miss Carolina, a 22 foot Pursuit with a T-Top for protection from the sun.

Shadow

Shadow is 27 foot PACEMAKER and she can hold up to 6 passengers. Shadow is captained by Tommy Bronski, who has been navigating the lowcountry waters for 35 years. When Tommmy is not on the sea he enjoys working on and welding on nuclear reactors.

Reel Justice

Reel Justice is a 27 foot CAROLINA SKIFF and she can hold up to 4 passengers. Captain Justice Adelman is the U.S. Coast Guard certified operator and owner of Reel Justice Charters. He has been fishing and navigating the waters off Hilton Head Island since he was ten years old. Captain Justice is passionate about fishing and helping make lasting memories for your family and friends

Hobo

The Hobo is a Coast Guard inspected 27 foot Carolina Skiff captained by Devon Christopher. The Hobo is the perfect choice for fishing with the entire family. With ample seating, high railings and a shaded area everyone will be comfortable as you fish the coastal waters of Hilton Head Island. The Hobo can hold up to 16 passengers.
