Before you go...

Please let your captain know in advance if you prefer a particular type of fishing or style of tackle.
They will be happy to accomodate you if possible.

What to Bring

  • Suntan lotion / Sunscreen
  • Hat (coverage for your face and neck)
  • Motion Sickness Medication (taken in time to be effective)
  • Food and Drinks (ice is provided)
  • Soft-soled deck shoes

What NOT to Bring

  • Hard liquor
    • (beer and wine are ok)
  • Black soled shoes
  • Large coolers
    • (coolers are supplied for drinks)
